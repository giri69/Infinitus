ABOUT INFINITUS'25

The 2024 edition of Infinitus raised the bar even higher in the realm of cultural festivities, bringing together an extraordinary gathering of over 12,000 students from diverse disciplines and backgrounds. This year’s event radiated unparalleled energy, with over 6,000 participants actively engaging in a wide range of competitions, performances, and showcases, exemplifying creativity, innovation, and collaboration. The digital outreach of Infinitus ’24 shattered all previous records, amassing over 20,000 interactions across social media platforms.

Attendees shared their thrilling experiences, creating a ripple effect of excitement that reached global audiences. The official website continued to thrive as a hub of real-time updates, drawing in over 7,000 visitors and reinforcing the festival's status as a highly anticipated event. Infinitus ’24 didn’t just celebrate cultures—it set a new precedent for creativity and excellence, leaving an indelible mark on the legacy of this grand fest. With its ambitious vision and execution, it truly paved the way for an even more vibrant future.

Director's Message

The 2024 edition of Infinitus marked a historical milestone in the legacy of cultural festivities, uniting an impressive assembly of 10,000 students from diverse backgrounds. The event witnessed an unprecedented level of enthusiasm, with over 5,000 participants actively engaging in various competitions and showcases, embodying the spirit of innovation and creativity. The digital footprint of Infinitus '23 expanded beyond expectations, achieving a remarkable outreach of 15,000 through social media platforms, where engagement soared as attendees shared their exhilarating experiences. The official website became a hotspot for information and updates, attracting over 5,000 visitors, a testament to the fest's growing popularity and the anticipation it builds each year. This chapter in the Infinitus saga not only celebrated the cultures but also set a new benchmark for future editions.

- Director Student Affairs
